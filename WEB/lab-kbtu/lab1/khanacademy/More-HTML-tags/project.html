Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or bacon 200g
Eggs 4
Grated Parmesan cheese 100g
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, fry pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and immediately toss in the egg mixture.
  5. Add fried pancetta or bacon and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with extra grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper on top.
